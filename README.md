## How to use
So, you want to use this extremely basic video editor?

Well I can give you ***VERY*** easy steps to do it **now**.

Starting off with how to

### Clear the timeline!
Look into the UI until you see this trash can image:

[Very ok trash can](/trash.png)

That trash can with an X is the Clear Timeline button.

If you want to clear your timeline because, for example, you want to make a new video, just press it!

Very simple software.

### Play a loaded video!
If you want to play a video, make sure the `
video` list isn't empty. That's your timeline!

Share them online(Probably)!

So now there's a different button for playing videos, as most other video editors.

Look for

[This](/play.png)

that button.

Press to play!

### Add photos!
It's not a video editor without some media!

Luckily, SNVE comes with some.

There are 2 images!

Press them to add that photo in your timeline.

Cool right?

Anyways they look like this or similar

[Wow mit](/mit.png)

[Cool grid](/gridit.png)

So just press them and see it in your timeline! They should look like `message2.1` or `message gd 2.3`.

Last but not least,

### Add transitions!
Transitions are very important part of every video so SNVE comes with only one!

There's a cool jelly with a T in it. Press it to add a transition!

It will automatically add a transition between the previous and the next (if any) media.

It looks like this:

[Jellyboy](/JELLYBEANZ.png)

### Time to make your first video!
First, prep your video by clearing the timeline.

This is important because if someone were to, I dunno, spam your timeline with images, you can clear it!

Second, add images and transitions!
Just do it in an order, whatever...

Third, play it just in case!

Lastly, export it.
How?

Easy!

Screen record it.
(Hehe!)

But first, hide the `video` list so you can record.

You can do this by pressing See inside and hiding it!

Now, you have your first video!
